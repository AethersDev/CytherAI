Nothing. This website makes zero external requests. No analytics, no tracking pixels, no cookies.

If you email us, we collect:

We use this only to respond to your inquiry and do not share it with third parties.

The CytherAI software itself makes zero external network requests. It runs entirely offline after deployment.
